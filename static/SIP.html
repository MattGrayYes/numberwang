You can use any SIP client to connect to the service,

  • Your SIP username is the phone number
  • The password is shown above.
  • The hostname is clients.sip.poc.emf.camp
  • Port 5060 either TCP or UDP.

For a mobile client we like Zoiper, you can download the app for your device using the buttons below and then scane the QR code with Zoiper to automatically setup your client. Zoiper users a push proxy service to keep the client connected, the Android Beta includes this for free, or for iOS you can get a 7 day free trial of the feature in app.
