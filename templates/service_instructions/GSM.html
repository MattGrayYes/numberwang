Install your SIM Card

  1. Pick up a SIM card from the shop on site.
  2. Insert it in your phone
  3. Wait for your phone to join the phone network named "99942" or "gsm.emf.camp"

Your phone should now be connected to our GSM network, but with a temporary number

How to activate this number on your handset

Option 1:

  1. Dial: 90201 to prove you're on the network. It'll read your temporary number back at you.
  2. Dial: 900 {{number.value}} {{number.param}} (this is one continuous number)
  3. Wait for confirmation that it has been applied successfully

Option 2:

  1. Dial 900
  2. When asked, enter your number: {{number.value}}
  3. When asked, enter your activation code: {{number.param}}
  4. Wait for confirmation that it has been applied successfuly
